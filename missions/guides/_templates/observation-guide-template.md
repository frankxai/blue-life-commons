# Observation Guide Template

> Copy this file to `missions/guides/<guide-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. An observation guide teaches how to watch or photograph wildlife **without harming it**. Ethics review is mandatory (`review.ethics: required`). The do-no-harm rules override everything else.

## What this guide is for

What the reader will be able to observe or document, and for whom.

## Ethical observation rules (do no harm)

This section overrides everything else.

- Minimum approach distance, with the regulation or guideline source.
- Signs of disturbance for the target species, with sources — and the rule: **if you see them, you are too close; leave.**
- Never touch, feed, bait, chase, or surround animals.
- Local regulations and permits: cite them.

## How to observe

Step-by-step, beginner-friendly. What to look for; how to position yourself; how to leave the site undisturbed.

## How to record

What to capture (species, count, behavior, conditions, date/time, regional location) and the recognized citizen-science platform to submit it to.

## Sources

- The full source list backing the distances, disturbance signs, and regulations above.
