# Scene Brief — One Record, End to End

## Creative thesis

The most cinematic thing the commons can show is not an ocean mood; it is a living species record whose evidence and rights survive inspection.

## Composition

- Left plane: commons proposition, actions, repository-derived counts.
- Right plane: approved whale shark image with truthful status badges.
- Evidence plane: record path, source count, media rights/checks, and reuse license.
- Links: public record, source image, and Git history.

## Motion

`source-trace` resolves the four evidence cells in causal order over 720ms total. It runs once. The animal does not move. Reduced-motion shows the final state immediately.

## Asset decision

Tier A product/science proof. The record, approved image, rights metadata, and render decision already exist in the repository. No new generated asset is required.

## Stop conditions

Stop if the image loses its credit/license, the record appears scientifically approved, the contribution path becomes commercial, or a preview exposes an unapproved candidate image.
