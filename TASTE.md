# Blue Life Commons Taste Contract

## Keep

- Open-commons, source-led, welfare-first, non-extractive posture.
- Clear scientific uncertainty and visible review status.
- Real animals shown at ethical distance with explicit rights and credit.
- Practical actions: inspect, reuse with attribution, contribute sources, review.

## Avoid

- SaaS pricing, lead funnels, dashboards as theater, conversion pressure, or gated knowledge.
- “Save the ocean” guilt, mystical animal claims, or unqualified conservation certainty.
- Generated wildlife presented as evidence, dark unidentified media, and unlabeled candidate assets.
- Exact vulnerable locations or interaction guidance without ethics review.

## Copy posture

Clear, warm, and useful. Confident about workflow; humble about science. Prefer “source-linked,” “review-gated,” and “approved media” to blanket claims that content is reviewed.
