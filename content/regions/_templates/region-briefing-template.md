# Region Briefing Template

> Copy this file to `content/regions/<region-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. A briefing covers local marine life, ecosystems, regulations, organizations, and how to engage responsibly — all sourced.

## Overview

Where this region is and why it matters for ocean life — two or three sentences. Regional granularity; never precise locations of vulnerable populations.

## Marine life to know

The flagship and indicator species of the region, linking to their species pages. What's seasonal, what's resident — cited.

## Ecosystems

Reefs, kelp, estuaries, open water — whatever defines this region, described plainly with sources.

## Protections and regulations

Marine protected areas, viewing rules, permits, and the authorities that set them. Cite the actual regulation. This section is the floor for any field mission in the region.

## Organizations and how to engage

Partner organizations active here (link partner profiles), citizen-science platforms, and reviewed field missions. How a visitor or resident can contribute responsibly.

## Seasonal calendar

What to look for and when — and the seasonal sensitivities to avoid (breeding, pupping, nesting, molting), cited.
