# Research Summary Template

> Copy this file to `content/research/<paper-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. Summarize a peer-reviewed paper for a general audience. Never extrapolate beyond what the paper claims. State limitations. `review.science: required`.

## The question

What the study set out to answer, in one or two plain sentences.

## What they did

Methods in brief — enough that a non-specialist understands the approach without jargon.

## What they found

The main findings, stated as the paper states them. Use the paper's own hedging; do not strengthen claims.

## Why it matters

The relevance for conservation, observation, or ocean understanding — without overreaching.

## Limitations

What the study does *not* show. Every summary names limitations; this is not optional.

## Sources

- The paper (DOI) and any directly relevant follow-ups, with access dates.
