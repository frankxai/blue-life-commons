# Event Report Template

> Copy this file to `content/events/<event-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. An event report records a sprint, workshop, field day, or contribution drive — what happened and what artifacts came out of it.

## What happened

The event: when, where (or online), who took part, and its purpose.

## Artifacts produced

The concrete outputs — species pages, missions, connectors, lessons — each linked. This is the heart of the report: an event is measured by what it shipped.

## What we learned

Honest reflection: what worked, what didn't, what to change next time.

## Credit

Contributors and their GitHub handles. Merged artifacts carry credit in their own metadata.

## Sources

- The event record and any reports referenced, with access dates.
