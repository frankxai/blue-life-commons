# Lesson Template

> Copy this file to `content/academy/<lesson-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. A lesson is built from already-reviewed commons content — link the species pages, briefings, and missions it draws on. Science claims inherit their citations from those artifacts.

## Learning outcomes

What a learner will know or be able to do after this lesson. Two to four concrete outcomes.

## Audience and level

Who it's for and what prior knowledge is assumed (beginner lessons assume none).

## The lesson

Short sections building understanding step by step. Define jargon on first use. Every factual claim links to its reviewed source artifact.

## Activity

A concrete, ethical activity — ideally tied to a reviewed field mission or observation guide. Never generate new wildlife-interaction guidance; link the reviewed one.

## Check understanding

A few questions or a reflection prompt.

## Sources

- The reviewed commons artifacts and external sources this lesson draws on.
