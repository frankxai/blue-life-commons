# Blue Life Commons Brand Guidelines

**Version:** 0.9 (2026-06-26) — Initial visual system seeded with Grok Imagine.
**Tone:** Warm. Precise. Hopeful. Practical. Source-led.

## Logo

### Core Lockups
- **Primary:** Horizontal wordmark "Blue Life Commons" with small tagline or icon to left.
- **Icon/Monogram:** Minimal distilled symbol (wave + life form, fluke, or current/compass).
- **Usage:**
  - Horizontal on most surfaces.
  - Icon-only for favicons, avatars, tight UI, merchandise.
  - Vertical for tall formats or stacked headers.

### Construction Rules
- Clear space: equal to height of "B" on all sides minimum.
- Never stretch, rotate, or add effects.
- On dark (preferred): light text + teal/gold accents.
- On light: deep teal or navy versions.
- Monochrome approved for single-color print.

### Do / Don't (to be expanded with real assets)
- Do: respect the deep ocean base + teal current.
- Do: pair with generous space and clean typography.
- Don't: place over busy photos without overlay.
- Don't: recreate from memory — use masters from brand/logos/.

## Color

See `brand/tokens/brand-tokens.css` for exact values and usage classes.

Core system:
- Deep bases for authority and underwater depth.
- Teal Current #4FD1C5 as signature (trust, clarity, life).
- Sky Light for secondary information and openness.
- Sunbreak Gold #F5B84B for hope, warmth, call to action, surface light.
- Life accents used sparingly for coral/healthy ecosystems.

Never use bright primary colors or pastels that feel toy-like. Test accessibility on both dark and light.

## Typography

Base from hero: Inter / system sans (Geist, Instrument Sans, etc.).

- Display / Headings: 700-850 weight, generous tracking on small caps or all-caps labels.
- Body: 400-600, excellent readability at 16-18px.
- Accent / Data: mono for IDs, codes, precise measurements.

## Imagery

### Photographic / Rendered Style
Premium cinematic nature + clean scientific illustration hybrid.

Key signatures:
- Strong natural light with warm golden surface rays or gentle god rays penetrating.
- Deep rich blues and teals, subtle bioluminescence accents.
- Subjects shown with dignity and space (ethical framing).
- People (when present): citizen scientists, quiet observers, notebook in hand, respectful boat-based or kayak.
- Composition: rule of thirds, breathing room, subtle data/grid references from hero for "commons" identity.

### Illustration / Infographic Style
- Clean modern with organic flow.
- High legibility text integrated as design (large, bold, limited words).
- Same palette lock.
- Minimal but elegant — never cluttered.
- For complex data: prefer code-rendered (HTML/CSS/SVG) over pure image gen for precision.

### Ethics in Visuals
- No images that could be mistaken for endorsement of close approach or harassment.
- Credit generated or note "Blue Life Commons generated concept" where appropriate.
- When using real reference photography later: only licensed or contributor-granted.

## Motion & Interactive (future)
- Slow, deliberate camera or parallax.
- Gentle current/wave motion.
- Light rays and particle life (plankton) subtle.
- Data reveals tied to user scroll or interaction (knowledge as gift).

## Application Examples
- Website hero: deep base + logo lockup + key art with warm light.
- Species page: portrait key art left or above, clean text, small icon badges.
- Field mission guide: map artistic + ethical icons + photo of respectful practice.
- Social: square crop of animal with minimal quote or fact + logo small.
- Presentations: dark slides, gold accents for emphasis.
