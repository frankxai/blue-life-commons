# Blue Life Commons Production Skill

Read `AGENTS.md`, `ETHICS.md`, `SOURCES.md`, `STYLE.md`, `DESIGN.md`, `TASTE.md`, and `SYSTEM.md` before public-site work.

- Treat repository metadata and approved render contracts as the source of truth.
- Every public factual claim must trace to an accepted source.
- Expose review status; never convert pending review into implied approval.
- Use approved licensed media only. Generated media is supporting concept art, not evidence.
- Preserve sensitive-location and animal-welfare constraints.
- Keep knowledge and reuse paths open; do not turn the commons into SaaS.
- Update the relevant design evidence record and verify a preview before handoff.
