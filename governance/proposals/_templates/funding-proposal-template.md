# Funding Proposal Template

> Copy this file to `governance/proposals/<proposal-slug>.md`, replace every section, and update the frontmatter. A funding proposal is a **fundable impact object**, not a vague donation ask — it names specific, evidenced work. See [governance/funding.md](../funding.md).

## What will be done

The specific work: a species guild, a region pilot, a researcher workflow, a connector, a sprint. Concrete and bounded.

## Why it matters

The gap this closes and who benefits (NGOs, researchers, coastal communities). Cite evidence of the need.

## Deliverables

The artifacts this produces — species pages, dataset cards, missions, connectors, lessons — each a reviewable commons artifact.

## Evidence and impact claim

How impact will be evidenced (merged PRs, published pages, partner feedback, usage). State the Hypercert-eligible impact claim precisely.

## Budget and steward

What is needed and who is accountable. Keep accounting separate by project (funding.md, Legal posture).

## Sources

- Supporting evidence for the need and scope, with access dates.
